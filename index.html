Privacy Policy

Effective date: 4 April 2026  ·  Last updated: 4 April 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Vaibhavam Guest House ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects information entered into the Vaibhavam Guest House Management System ("the App"). The App is an internal management tool used exclusively by authorised staff of Vaibhavam Guest House to manage bookings, billing, and property operations.

1. Who This App Is For

The Vaibhavam Guest House Management System is a private, staff-only application. It is not a public-facing booking portal. Access is restricted to employed staff members of Vaibhavam Guest House who have been granted login credentials by the property owner.

Guests of the property do not directly interact with this application. Their information is entered into the system by staff on their behalf at the time of booking.

2. Information We Collect

The App collects two categories of information:

Category What is collected Why
Guest information Full name, phone number, email address, home address, government ID type and number (optional) To create and manage booking records and invoices
Booking data Check-in and check-out dates, units booked, payment amounts, advance payments, balance due To manage reservations and generate invoices
Financial records Invoice amounts, payment method (cash/UPI/bank transfer), payment stage, references To track payments and produce financial reports
Operational data Property expenses (electricity, wages, maintenance), expense categories, referral sources To track running costs and analyse marketing effectiveness
Staff account data Staff full name, email address, username, phone number, role To manage access control for property staff

We do not collect payment card numbers, CVV codes, or any full banking credentials. Payment method type is recorded (e.g. "UPI") but no sensitive financial account details are stored.

3. How We Use Information

All information collected is used solely for the following purposes:

  • Creating and managing guest booking records
  • Generating itemised invoices and tracking payments
  • Managing unit availability and property operations
  • Tracking property expenses and generating financial reports for the owner
  • Identifying which marketing channels bring guests (referral source tracking)
  • Managing staff access and login credentials

Information is not used for advertising, profiling, or any purpose outside of property management operations.

4. We Do Not Sell Your Data

✓ We do not sell, rent, trade, or share guest or staff information with any third party for commercial purposes. Ever.

5. Data Sharing

Data entered into the App is accessible only to authorised staff of Vaibhavam Guest House based on their assigned role:

  • Front Desk Staff — can view bookings, guests, and billing for operational use
  • Manager — same as Front Desk, plus expense management and discounts
  • Owner — full access including financial reports and staff management

We use the following third-party infrastructure services to operate the App. These services process data on our behalf under their own privacy policies:

  • Supabase (supabase.com) — database, authentication, and backend services. Data is stored in the South Asia (Mumbai) region.
  • Vercel (vercel.com) — web application hosting.
  • Expo / EAS (expo.dev) — mobile application build and update delivery.

These providers do not have independent access to your data for their own purposes. They act solely as infrastructure providers.

6. Data Storage and Security

  • All data is transmitted over HTTPS — encrypted in transit at all times
  • Data is stored in Supabase's managed PostgreSQL database hosted in Mumbai, India
  • Access is protected by JWT-based authentication — staff must log in with valid credentials
  • Role-based access control (RLS) is enforced at the database level — staff can only access data appropriate to their role
  • Passwords are hashed by Supabase Auth — we never store plain-text passwords
  • Staff sessions expire after inactivity and require re-login

While we take reasonable technical measures to protect data, no system is completely immune to security risks. In the event of a data breach, affected individuals will be notified promptly.

7. Data Retention

Booking records, invoices, and payment histories are retained indefinitely for financial record-keeping and audit purposes. This is standard practice for hospitality businesses.

Guest profiles can be deactivated upon request. Staff accounts are deactivated (not deleted) when employment ends, preserving historical records linked to their actions.

No data is ever permanently deleted from our database — records are marked as inactive while remaining in place for audit continuity.

8. Your Rights

Guests whose information has been entered into the system may contact us to:

  • Request a copy of their personal information held in the system
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of their guest profile (subject to legal retention requirements)

Staff members may request access to or correction of their own account information through the property owner.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the details in Section 11.

9. Children's Privacy

This application is not intended for use by children under the age of 18. The App is a professional business management tool used exclusively by adult staff members. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or applicable law. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.

We encourage staff to review this policy periodically. Continued use of the App after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
